# Pricing & Subscriptions

AMP is built to be sustainable while remaining affordable for indie developers. Our pricing model scales with your game's success.

## Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | Matches/Day | Support | Ideal For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hobby / Dev** | $0 | 100 | Community | Prototypes, Hackathons |
| **Indie** | $49/mo | 5,000 | Email | Small PvP Games |
| **Studio** | $199/mo | 50,000 | Priority / Discord | Established Titles |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Dedicated / SLA | Flagship Avalanche Games |

---

## What's Included?

- **Matchmaking Infra:** Low-latency pairing of players.
- **Escrow Contracts:** Secure handling of wagers (AVAX/ERC-20).
- **Verifier Certifications:** Signed attestations for every match.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Visibility into match history, desync rates, and player MMRs.

## Settlement Fees

In addition to the subscription, a small **Protocol Fee** (e.g., 0.5% - 1%) may be taken from the match wager at the time of settlement to fund the Verifier network and ongoing development. This is configurable per game in the `AMPRegistry`.

## Assumptions & FAQ

- **Does "Indie" include Fuji and Mainnet?** Yes, both networks are supported under the same tier.
- **What happens if I exceed my daily limit?** Matches will continue to be processed, but you will be billed a small overage fee per 1,000 matches.
- **Can I pay in AVAX?** Yes, we support both USDC and AVAX payments.
